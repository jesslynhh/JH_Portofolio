Social Media Analytic: Comparative Study of Public Transportation in Jakarta and Semarang

Looker Studio

In my recent study, accepted for presentation at a conference, my team developed a dashboard to analyze public sentiment towards public transportation services in Jakarta and Semarang, focusing on Transjakarta and Trans Semarang, using social media data. By integrating BERTopic and sentiment classification models, the dashboard provides insights into sentiment trends, key topics, and tweet distributions. The study revealed that public sentiment towards Trans Semarang was more positive compared to Transjakarta, with the system achieving strong performance metrics, including a sentiment classification accuracy of 78% and high user satisfaction. My contributions included developing the dashboard, conducting literature review research, and co-authoring the research paper.
